The story

A place that feels like home.

Blessed African Market opened its doors in Norcross, Georgia in 2013 with a simple mission — make it easy for African families in the Atlanta area to find the foods and ingredients they grew up with.

Over a decade later, we've become more than just a grocery store. We're a gathering place. A spot where you run into your neighbor, catch up, and leave with everything you need for a real home-cooked meal.

We source authentic products from across West Africa and the diaspora — from everyday staples like palm oil and stockfish, to harder-to-find items you won't see at a typical supermarket.
